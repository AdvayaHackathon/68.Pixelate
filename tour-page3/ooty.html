ooty hill station

Ooty, officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a renowned hill station nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. Situated at an elevation of approximately 2,240 meters (7,347 feet) above sea level, it is often referred to as the "Queen of Hill Stations" due to its picturesque landscapes and pleasant climate.
